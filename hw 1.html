World Wide Web

The World Wide Web (W3) is a wide-area hypermedia information retrieval initiative aiming to give universal access to a large universe of documents.

Everything there is online about W3 is linked or indirectly to this document, including an executive summary of the project, Mailing lists,Policy, November's W3 news,Frequently Asked Questions.
